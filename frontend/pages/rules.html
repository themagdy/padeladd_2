PadelAdd Rules

How scoring, ranking, and eligibility work.

🏆 Match Types

Competition

Affects points and ranking. Restricted to players within ±100 pts of the creator.

Friendly

Does not affect ranking. Open to players within ±300 pts of the creator.

🧮 Scoring Formula

Points are awarded based on match results, team strength difference, and win intensity.

Base Result ±4 pts
Strength Adjustment 0 to ±3 pts
Heavy Win (Game diff ≥7) ±1 pt
Multipliers
  • New Player (0-15 matches): Multiplier of 1.5x to 1.2x
  • Integrity Factor: Points can be reduced if playing the same opponents too often.

✅ Result Confirmation

1
Winning team submits score

After the match ends, the winning team records the score.

2
24-hour verification window

Opponents have 24 hours to confirm or dispute. No action results in auto-confirmation.
