Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Padeladd. We are committed to protecting your personal data and your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our web and mobile applications.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (Name, Email, Phone Number)
  • Profile details (Nickname, Gender, Skill Level, Profile Image)
  • Match data (Matches created, joined, and results submitted)
  • Communication data (Messages sent through our in-app chat)

3. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to provide and improve the Padeladd experience, specifically to:

  • Facilitate match scheduling and player coordination
  • Calculate and maintain your ranking and performance stats
  • Enable communication between match participants
  • Send important notifications about your matches and account
  • Ensure platform integrity and fair play

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your nickname, skill level, and ranking are visible to other players to facilitate match-making. Your phone number is only shared with match participants when explicitly requested and approved by you.

5. Mobile Specific Data

Our mobile application may request access to:

  • Push Notifications: To alert you about match requests, chat messages, and score approvals.
  • Camera/Gallery: To allow you to upload a profile picture.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time through your profile settings. For any data-related requests that cannot be handled through the app, please contact us.
