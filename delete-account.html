Padeladd Account Deletion

How to request the deletion of your account and associated data

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the Padeladd mobile application by following these steps:

  1. Open the Padeladd app and log in.
  2. Go to the Profile tab (the profile icon in the navigation bar).
  3. Tap on Edit Profile.
  4. Scroll down to the bottom of the page to find the Danger Zone.
  5. Tap on Delete Account and confirm your request.

Option 2: Web Deletion Request Form

If you have uninstalled the app or are unable to log in, you can request account deletion by filling out this form. We will process your request within 7 business days.

Warning: Deleting your account is permanent. All your data including matches, profile settings, and history will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

Data Retention Policies

Upon receiving an account deletion request (whether through the app or this form):

  • Personal Data: Your name, email, phone number, and profile details are completely scrubbed from our active databases.
  • Match History & Bookings: Your name will be removed from all past matches and displayed as "Deleted User" to protect the history of other participants.
  • Backups: Residual copies of your data may remain in secure backup systems for up to 30 days before being completely overwritten.
