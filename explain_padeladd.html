HOW PADELADD WORKS

Your guide to creating matches, submitting scores, and rising to the top of the local ranking ladder.

Welcome to Padeladd! We've built a modern, automated ladder system that connects padel players, facilitates match bookings, and tracks statistics in real-time. Whether you are looking for friendly practice rounds or intense ranked competitions, Padeladd keeps the court active and exciting.

3 Steps to Get Started

1

Create or Join a Challenge

Set up a match (Ranked or Friendly) with a target venue, date, and time, or browse active court bookings on your dashboard to jump into open spots.

2

Play & Log Your Scores

Hit the court! After the match, submit the game scores directly from your player portal. The opposing team has 24 hours to review and confirm the result.

3

Rankings Automatically Adjust

Once verified, rankings are calculated instantly. Point allocations reflect base outcomes, opponent levels, and domination bonuses.

Points Breakdown

Ranked matches impact your position on the ladder. Adjustments are derived using the following factors:

Factor Points Impact
Base Result: Standard gain/loss points for a match. ± 6 pts
Opponent Strength: Bonus points earned for defeating higher-ranked teams. ± 0-9 pts
Domination Bonus: Awarded for winning a match by a margin of 8+ games. + 4 pts
New Player Calibration: Multiplier during your first 20 matches. 2.0x Boost
💡 Pro Tip: To keep competitions fair, point adjustments degrade if you challenge the exact same players repeatedly within a short window.
